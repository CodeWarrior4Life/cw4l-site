Extension Janitor scans your browser for orphaned extension entries, heavyweight extensions, and stale caches. The Extension offers both free and paid tiers:

The native cleanup component operates outside the browser to remove orphaned entries from browser configuration files. This requires a separately installed companion application that runs with your explicit authorization.
